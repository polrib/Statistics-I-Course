Best Bayesian Counterpart. To build the BBC we must find the best estimators for our parameters mu and sigma1. We know from the Normal and Gamma distributions that their mean has the highest probability, so they are the best estimators we can obtain. From the summary of mcfit, we got the means that enabled us to represent the best estimators.
